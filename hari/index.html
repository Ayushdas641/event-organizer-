Football

Football (soccer) is the world’s most popular sport, played by two teams of 11 players each on a rectangular field with a goal at each end.

Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of 11 players each on a circular or oval field, with a rectangular 22-yard pitch at its center.

Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played on a rectangular court, where two teams of five players each aim to score points by shooting a ball through the opposing team's hoop.
